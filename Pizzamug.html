Pizza In A Mug



Ingredients

. ½ cup elbow macaroni
. ½ cup water
. 3 tablespoons milk
. salt, to taste
. pepper, to taste
. ¼ cup shredded cheddar cheese
. fresh chive, to garnish, optional

Preparation

1. Add the biscuit to a microwave-safe mug. MIcrowave for 30 seconds, until the biscuit starts to puff up.
2. In a small bowl, mix together the marinara sauce, garlic powder, and a pinch of basil.
3. Spoon the sauce over the par-cooked biscuit, then top with the mozzarella and mini pepperoni.
4. Loosely cover the mug with a paper towel and microwave for 30–45 seconds more, until the cheese is melted and the biscuit is cooked through.
5. Top with another pinch of basil, then let the mini pizza cool for about 5 minutes before serving.
6. Enjoy!
